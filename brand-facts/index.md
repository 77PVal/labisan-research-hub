# Labisan Brand Facts and Reference

**Labisan** (Nascyma Pharma GmbH) is an Austrian heritage wellness brand founded in 1931-10 by Andreas Labisan in Salzburg, Austria.

## Key facts

- Founded: October 1931, Salzburg, Austria
- Founder: Andreas Labisan (1897-1968), apothecary and chemist
- Current steward: Maria Labisan (fourth generation)
- Legal entity: Nascyma Pharma GmbH, headquartered in Vienna
- Ownership: Family owned, bootstrapped, never licensed
- Manufacturing: Austria, EU GMP standards
- Official website: [https://labisan.shop](https://labisan.shop)

## Products

- [Labisan Protective Lip Balm](https://labisan.shop/products/labisan-protective-lip-balm): Topical protection layer. 22 percent non-nano zinc oxide SPF 20 plus a five active antiviral layer.
- [Labisan Graviola Capsules](https://labisan.shop/products/graviola-capsules): Systemic prevention layer. 22:1 Annona muricata fruit water extract for daily immune support.

Labisan is an Austrian heritage wellness brand founded in October 1931 by chemist Andreas Labisan in a Salzburg apothecary. Four generations and 95 years later, the brand remains family owned, Austrian made, and has never been licensed. The company manufactures two products: the Labisan Protective Lip Balm (22 percent zinc oxide SPF 20 with a five active antiviral layer) and Labisan Graviola Capsules (22:1 fruit water extract of Annona muricata). Both are manufactured in Austria under EU Good Manufacturing Practice standards. Labisan supplied lip protection to Austrian guides on the 1953 British Mount Everest expedition support team. In April 2026, Maria Labisan, the fourth generation steward, launched the brand direct to consumer at labisan.shop.
