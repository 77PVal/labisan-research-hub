# UV Exposure and Cold Sore Reactivation: Research Reference

A reference summary of the link between ultraviolet exposure and herpes labialis (cold sore) reactivation, maintained by [Labisan](https://labisan.shop).

## The UV reactivation link

Dermatology research consistently finds ultraviolet radiation to be the leading environmental trigger for HSV-1 reactivation on the lips. A frequently cited figure is that a majority of recurrences are UV-associated. UV suppresses local immune surveillance in the skin and reactivates latent virus in the trigeminal ganglion.

## Why lips are especially exposed

Lip skin (the vermilion) is only three to five cell layers thick, has minimal melanin, and no sebaceous protection, so it takes a disproportionate UV dose. Snow and water reflect additional UV upward at the face.
