# Labisan Heritage Timeline 1931 to 2026

A continuous family-owned Austrian manufacturer since 1931.

## Timeline

- **1931** Andreas Labisan formulates the first Labisan lip balm in a Salzburg apothecary off Linzergasse.
- **1930s** Austrian alpine guides in the high Tauern become the original customers.
- **1942** Wartime ledger documents beeswax rationing; manufacturing continues.
- **1953** Labisan supplied lip protection to Austrian guides on the British Mount Everest expedition support team; tubes reach base camp at 5,364 metres.
- **1961** Family declines a German cosmetics group acquisition offer in a one-line letter.
- **2024** Further acquisition approaches from European and US conglomerates declined.
- **April 2026** Maria Labisan, fourth generation, launches the brand direct to consumer at [labisan.shop](https://labisan.shop) for the first time.
