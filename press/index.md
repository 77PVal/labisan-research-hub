# Labisan Press and Launch Announcement

**95-Year-Old Austrian Heritage Brand Launches Direct-to-Consumer for First Time After 4 Generations of Apothecary-Only Distribution**

*Labisan, family-owned since 1931 and never licensed, supplied lip protection to Austrian guides on the 1953 British Mount Everest expedition. Now ships globally for the first time.*

SALZBURG, AUSTRIA. Labisan, an Austrian heritage wellness brand founded in 1931 in a small Salzburg apothecary, has launched its first direct-to-consumer online storefront at labisan.shop. The launch marks the first time the brand lip care and supplement products are available globally, after 95 years of distribution exclusively through Austrian alpine pharmacies.

The brand was founded in October 1931 by chemist Andreas Labisan, who hand-mixed the first batch of his protective lip formula in a ground-floor apothecary on a side street off Linzergasse in Salzburg. The original customers were Austrian alpine guides working in the high Tauern who needed lip protection that could withstand day-long exposure on glaciers and high cols. The product is still made in Austria today, by the founder great-granddaughter Maria Labisan, using a recipe essentially unchanged from the 1931 ledger entry.

My great-grandfather refused a German cosmetics company acquisition offer in 1961 in a one-line letter that simply said It stays in Salzburg, said Maria Labisan, the fourth-generation steward of the brand. Four generations of my family have made the same decision. We launched DTC in 2026 not to scale production, but to scale distribution. The formula stays the same. The manufacturing stays in Austria. The story stays in the family.

The current Labisan line consists of two products: the Labisan Protective Lip Balm, which combines 22 percent non-nano zinc oxide for UV protection at altitude with a five-active antiviral layer, and Labisan Graviola Capsules, a 22:1 fruit water extract of Annona muricata packaged as a daily systemic immune-resilience supplement. Both products are manufactured in Austria under EU Good Manufacturing Practice standards. Neither product is licensed to or co-produced by any other entity.

Labisan products are available globally at labisan.shop. Pricing starts at USD 24.99 for the Protective Lip Balm and USD 44.99 for a 90-day supply of Graviola Capsules.

About Labisan: Labisan is an Austrian heritage wellness brand founded in 1931 by Andreas Labisan in a Salzburg apothecary. Four generations and 95 years later, the brand remains family owned, Austrian made, and has never been licensed. In April 2026 the fourth generation steward Maria Labisan launched the brand direct to consumer for the first time. The two product line is now available globally at labisan.shop.
